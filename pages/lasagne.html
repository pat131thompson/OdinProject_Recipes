Lasagne

A hearty Italian Lasagne dish!

You can go to the Home Page or follow the links to find recipes for Enchiladas or Ramen.


Ingredients:

  • Lasagne sheets
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer 10 mins.
  3. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish. Layer lasagne sheets, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  4. Cover with foil and bake 25 mins. Remove foil, bake 10 more mins until bubbly and golden. Let rest 10 mins before serving
