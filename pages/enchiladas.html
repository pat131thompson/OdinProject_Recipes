Enchiladas

An exciting Mexican Enchilada recipe!

You can go to the Home Page or follow the links to find recipes for Lasagne or Ramen.


Ingredients:

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • Sour cream and cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix chicken with half the enchilada sauce and onions.
  3. Soften tortillas by warming in a pan or microwave. Fill each with chicken mixture, roll up, and place seam side down in a baking dish.
  4. Pour remaining sauce over rolled tortillas and sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes until cheese melts and edges are crispy. Garnish with sour cream and cilantro.
