Ramen

A steaming bowl of Ramen!

You can go to the Home Page or follow the links to find recipes for Lasagne or Enchiladas.


Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 packs instant ramen noodles (discard seasoning packets)
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup spinach or bok choy
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Soy sauce, sesame oil, and chili flakes to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring broth to a simmer. Add mushrooms and cook 5 minutes.
  2. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender (about 3 mins).
  3. Stir in spinach, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Cook until wilted.
  4. Serve in bowls topped with halved boiled eggs, green onions, and chili flakes.
